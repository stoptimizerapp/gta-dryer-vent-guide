Transparent by design

A local guide and a clear handoff

GTA Dryer Vent Guide helps people describe a dryer vent request and connect with a participating local provider. It is not a dryer vent contractor.

What we do

Useful information before the sales call

The site explains access, scope, maintenance guidance and questions to ask. When a visitor opts in, we may send their request to one participating provider that covers the area and property type.

That provider contacts the visitor directly and is solely responsible for confirming credentials, insurance, availability, inspection method, scope, price, workmanship and warranties.

What we do not do

  • We do not perform cleaning, inspection or repair work.
  • We do not employ or supervise participating providers.
  • We do not operate an emergency service.
  • We do not guarantee availability, outcomes or search rankings.
  • We do not represent a city, fire service or government agency.

Editorial team

GTA Dryer Vent Guide Editorial Team

This organizational byline identifies the team responsible for researching, writing and reviewing the site. It is not a contractor persona and does not imply trade credentials or first-hand service experience.

Primary-source research

Safety and maintenance statements are checked against official sources such as Toronto Fire Services, Health Canada and Natural Resources Canada. Price guides use dated, publicly posted GTA provider pricing and link to each source.

Editorial review

Articles are reviewed for source support, local relevance, referral disclosure and clear separation between cleaning, repair and emergency advice. Each article shows its latest review date.

No invented proof

The site does not invent reviews, completed jobs, certifications, prices, business addresses or field observations. Provider claims are attributed rather than presented as our own experience.

Evidence status and corrections

Our current original work is desk research: comparing public GTA prices, scopes and access conditions across providers. We do not yet publish original onsite measurements, job photos or technician interviews. If field evidence is added, it will identify the contributor, date, method and any commercial relationship.

Pages are rechecked when material information changes. Until a direct editorial contact is added, source corrections can be documented through the site repository.

Provider-partner standards

Minimum requirements before a provider receives requests

A future participating provider must agree to a clear consumer handoff. These standards do not replace the homeowner’s own checks.

Identifiable and insured

The provider must disclose its legal business identity, service area and current liability-insurance details for confirmation before requests are routed.

Scope before authorization

The provider must confirm the inspection or cleaning scope, taxes, access charges, exclusions and final price directly with the customer before work begins.

Consent-limited contact

Lead information may be used only to respond to the requested dryer-vent service. It may not be sold, reposted or used for unrelated marketing.

Current status: Provider participation, availability and suitability are not guaranteed. Visitors should independently confirm insurance, experience, reviews and written terms before booking.

Commercial disclosure

How this site can be funded

GTA Dryer Vent Guide may receive compensation from a participating provider for marketing and referral services. A visitor is not charged by this site to submit a request. Any provider price is arranged directly between the visitor and provider.

Important: Participation does not replace your own checks. Ask the provider for current insurance, relevant experience, a written scope and the final price before authorizing work.
