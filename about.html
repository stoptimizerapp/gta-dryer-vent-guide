Transparent by design

A local guide and a clear handoff

GTA Dryer Vent Guide helps people describe a dryer vent request and connect with a participating local provider. It is not a dryer vent contractor.

What we do

Useful information before the sales call

The site explains access, scope, maintenance guidance and questions to ask. When a visitor opts in, we may send their request to one participating provider that covers the area and property type.

That provider contacts the visitor directly and is solely responsible for confirming credentials, insurance, availability, inspection method, scope, price, workmanship and warranties.

What we do not do

  • We do not perform cleaning, inspection or repair work.
  • We do not employ or supervise participating providers.
  • We do not operate an emergency service.
  • We do not guarantee availability, outcomes or search rankings.
  • We do not represent a city, fire service or government agency.

Editorial approach

How information is prepared

Official sources

Safety and maintenance statements point to guidance from Toronto Fire Services, Health Canada and Natural Resources Canada.

No invented proof

The site does not publish fake reviews, jobs completed, accreditations, prices, business addresses or service claims.

Useful local detail

Area pages focus on building and access differences rather than changing only a city name.

Commercial disclosure

How this site can be funded

GTA Dryer Vent Guide may receive compensation from a participating provider for marketing and referral services. A visitor is not charged by this site to submit a request. Any provider price is arranged directly between the visitor and provider.

Important: Participation does not replace your own checks. Ask the provider for current insurance, relevant experience, a written scope and the final price before authorizing work.

Questions about this website can be sent to hello@gtadryerventguide.ca. This address must be activated before public launch.
