Research date: July 16, 2026  
Market: Toronto, Ontario / Greater Toronto Area  
Languages checked: English  

## Why this niche was selected

Google Keyword Planner showed enough demand to support a focused local site while location and property-type terms create lower-volume entry points. Ahrefs' free checker rated `dryer vent cleaning Toronto` at Keyword Difficulty 14 (Medium), estimating that roughly 16 referring websites may be needed to compete for the top 10. This is not a ranking guarantee; the live results still include ads, a local pack and established providers.

## On-page and technical implementation

- One unique title, description, canonical URL and H1 per indexable page.
- Semantic internal links among city, service and informational pages.
- `Organization`, `WebSite`, `WebPage`, `Article`, `BreadcrumbList` and honest `FAQPage` structured data.
- Visible organizational author/reviewer attribution and a linked editorial-methodology section.
- A dated five-provider GTA pricing study with source links, scope notes and evidence limits.
- Open Graph and large-card social metadata with a branded sharing image.
- No `LocalBusiness` schema, fake address, fake reviews, fake phone number or fake service history.
- XML sitemap and robots file.
- Responsive CSS, keyboard focus, skip links, reduced-motion support and labelled forms.
- Netlify-compatible lead form with required referral consent and spam honeypot.
- Safety claims linked to Toronto Fire Services, Health Canada and Natural Resources Canada.

## Authority plan for the first 90 days

1. Register and deploy the final `.ca` domain, verify Search Console and submit the sitemap.
2. Sign one legitimate contractor partner before enabling lead delivery; document service area, insurance confirmation process, response hours and excluded job types.
3. Ask relevant local organizations for earned links: condo-management resources, realtor maintenance guides, landlord resources, home-inspection partners and neighbourhood publications. Do not buy low-quality link packages.
4. Publish two genuinely useful local articles per month based on Search Console queries and real provider questions.
5. Add real provider information only after written permission, and date-check insurance/credential claims.
6. Improve titles and internal links after enough Search Console impressions exist; do not create dozens of thin city pages.

## Measurement

- Organic sessions and non-brand Search Console impressions.
- Quote-form starts, valid submissions and provider-confirmed contact rate.
- Qualified request rate by city and property type.
- Referring domains and links to the most useful guides.
- Page experience and indexing coverage.

Do not judge SEO on form submissions in the first few weeks. A new domain normally needs indexing, content discovery and authority development before stable organic results appear.
