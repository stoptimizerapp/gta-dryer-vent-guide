Privacy Policy

This policy explains what GTA Dryer Vent Guide collects and how information from a quote request is handled.

Launch requirement: The site owner should have this policy reviewed for the final hosting, analytics, CRM, provider agreement and business identity before public launch.

Information we collect

When you submit a quote request, we collect the information you choose to provide, which may include your name, email address, phone number, city, postal code, property type, preferred timing, dryer-vent concern and access details.

Our hosting and security services may also process technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, requested pages, timestamps and form-security signals.

How we use information

  • To review and route an opted-in request to a participating provider
  • To communicate about the request or respond to a question
  • To protect the site and reduce spam or misuse
  • To understand site performance and improve content
  • To meet legal, accounting or security obligations where applicable

How quote requests are shared

With your consent, we may share the request and contact information with one participating local provider so that provider can contact you about the requested service. The provider has its own privacy practices. We do not authorize a provider to use your information for unrelated marketing.

Service providers

Information may be processed by companies that provide website hosting, form handling, email, analytics, security or customer-relationship tools. The final list depends on the services configured at launch and should be disclosed here by the site owner.

Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the quote request, operations, security and applicable record-keeping. The site owner must set and document a specific retention schedule before launch.

Cookies and analytics

This static build does not include advertising cookies or analytics by default. If analytics, call tracking, remarketing or other tracking is added, this section and the consent experience must be updated before that technology is enabled.

Your choices

You may ask about personal information associated with a quote request, request a correction, or withdraw consent for future handling where applicable. A withdrawal does not reverse information already shared with a provider; contact that provider about its copy.

Security

No online method is completely secure. The site owner should use HTTPS, access controls, multi-factor authentication and a form provider with appropriate safeguards.

Children

This website and quote process are intended for adults arranging property services, not children.

Contact

Email privacy questions to privacy@gtadryerventguide.ca. This mailbox must be activated and monitored before public launch.

Changes

We may update this policy as tools and practices change. The current date will appear at the top of this page.
